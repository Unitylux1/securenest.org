How Does Ripple Technology Ensure the Security and Privacy of Global Transactions?

Ripple technology ensures the security and privacy of global transactions by utilizing advanced cryptographic techniques. It employs a decentralized network, validating transactions in real-time, and encrypts sensitive information, making it nearly impossible for hackers to compromise your data.

Can Ripple Be Used for Micropayments and Small Transactions?

Yes, Ripple can be used for micropayments and small transactions. It offers fast, low-cost transactions, making it ideal for everyday purchases. In fact, Ripple’s network can process up to 1,500 transactions per second, ensuring quick and efficient payments.
