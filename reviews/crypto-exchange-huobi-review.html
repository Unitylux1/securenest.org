What Is Huobi?

Huobi is a big name in the world of crypto trading. It is a place where you can buy and sell over 606 different types of digital money like Bitcoin. People from many countries use Huobi to trade cryptocurrencies.

Its goal is to create a safe space for people who want to invest in cryptocurrencies. The exchange makes it easy for you to pay with different methods when buying or selling your digital coins.

History of Huobi

Leon Li started Huobi in 2013. He worked for Oracle before this. First, he got the Huobi domain and then began live Bitcoin trading. At first, the firm was based in China. But later, it moved to Seychelles because of rules in China.

The company grew quite fast. It opened offices in Hong Kong, South Korea, Japan and the United States too. The year 2023 was hard for Huobi as laws became stricter around crypto exchanges like it across many countries.

This led to job cuts where they had to let go of one-fifth of their staff.

Overview of Huobi Mobile App

The Huobi Mobile App is a handy tool for crypto trading. You can find it on both Android and iOS stores. This app has a sleek design that’s easy to use. It lets you make an account, add money, and buy or sell different types of cryptocurrencies right from your phone.

Huobi Security & Safety

Huobi takes security and safety seriously to protect its users’ funds. They have implemented two-factor authentication (2FA) as an extra layer of security for account access. The exchange also uses data encryption and stores most user funds in offline cold wallets, which adds an extra level of protection against online threats.

In case of any asset loss not caused by the users themselves, Huobi has a Safety Provisions program in place to compensate affected individuals. While there have been regulatory concerns about the legality of Huobi as a cryptocurrency exchange, they continue to prioritize security measures to ensure their users’ assets are safe.

Services Offered by Huobi Exchange

Huobi Exchange offers a wide range of services for cryptocurrency traders. Users can trade various digital assets on the platform, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and many others. Additionally, Huobi provides an overview of its native token, Huobi Token (HT), which offers additional benefits to users.

Trading on Huobi Exchange

Trading on Huobi Exchange is easy and convenient. You can trade a wide range of cryptocurrencies on both the spot market and the margin and derivatives market. Huobi supports multiple fiat currencies, so you can trade using USD, GBP, AUD, EUR, and more.

The exchange accepts various payment methods, making it accessible for users around the world. If you’re new to trading or just want a quick way to buy cryptocurrencies, Huobi also offers a quick buy option.

With its user-friendly interface and extensive trading options, Huobi Exchange provides a seamless experience for traders of all levels.

Overview of Huobi Token (HT)

Huobi Token (HT) is the cryptocurrency native to the Huobi exchange. It has several uses within the platform. Firstly, HT can be used to reduce trading fees on the Huobi exchange, making it more cost-effective for users.

HT can be received as a form of payment for services offered on the Huobi platform. Moreover, HT token holders have the ability to vote for new tokens that they would like to see listed on Huobi.

HT can also be used to pay for service-fee packages provided by Huobi. Overall, Huobi Token (HT) plays a significant role in enhancing user experience and providing additional benefits and functionalities for traders on the Huobi platform.

Huobi Exchange Review: Pros and Cons

Huobi Exchange has several advantages that make it a popular choice among traders. It offers a wide range of cryptocurrencies for trading, provides robust security measures to protect user funds, and has a user-friendly mobile app.

However, there are also some drawbacks to consider, such as high withdrawal fees and limited payment options.

Advantages of Using Huobi Exchange

Huobi Exchange has several advantages that make it a popular choice among traders. Here are some of the key advantages:

  1. Deep liquidity: Huobi Global offers deep liquidity in leading cryptocurrency trading pairs, ensuring there are ample buyers and sellers for efficient trading.
  2. Extensive range of cryptocurrencies: Huobi supports over 700 cryptocurrencies, providing traders with a wide variety of options for their investment needs.
  3. User-friendly mobile app: Huobi has a well-designed and intuitive mobile trading app with a user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to trade on the go.
  4. Competitive trading fees: Compared to other exchanges, Huobi offers competitive trading fees, with a 0.2% maker fee and a 0.2% taker fee, allowing users to save on transaction costs.
  5. Robust security measures: Huobi prioritizes the safety of its users’ funds by implementing robust security measures such as cold storage and two-factor authentication (2FA), providing an extra layer of security.

Disadvantages of Using Huobi Exchange

Using Huobi Exchange also comes with a few disadvantages that you should be aware of:

  1. Counterparty risks: There is a potential vulnerability to risks such as hacking, theft, and insolvency when using Huobi Exchange. This means that there is a possibility of losing your funds or having them compromised by third parties.
  2. Lack of demo account: Huobi Exchange does not offer a demo account for users to practice trading. This can make it challenging for beginners or those who want to familiarize themselves with the platform before investing real money.
  3. Limited customer support: Some users have reported that customer support on Huobi Exchange can be slow and unresponsive at times. This can make it difficult to get timely assistance or resolve any issues that may arise.
  4. Lengthy verification process: The verification process on Huobi Exchange can be time-consuming and require a lot of personal information. This may be inconvenient for users who value privacy or want to start trading quickly.
  5. Limited selection of cryptocurrencies and tokens: While Huobi Exchange supports a variety of digital currencies, the selection is still relatively limited compared to other exchanges. This means that you may not be able to trade all the cryptocurrencies or tokens you are interested in.
  6. Safety and security concerns: Like any centralized exchange, there is always a risk associated with storing your funds on an online platform. Despite the robust security measures in place, the unregulated nature of the exchange might raise concerns for some users.

Huobi Exchange Registration Process

To sign up on Huobi Exchange, simply visit their website and click on the “Sign Up” button. Fill out the required information, including your email address and password. Once you have completed the registration form, a verification email will be sent to your inbox.

Click on the verification link in the email to activate your account and start trading on Huobi Exchange.

Sign Up on Huobi Exchange

To sign up on Huobi Exchange, you can either visit their website or download the app. Look for the “Sign Up” button in the middle or upper right corner of the page. During registration, you will have to provide your email address or mobile number to create a Huobi account.

If you have a referral code, you can use it during this process. Huobi Exchange accepts various payment methods, making it convenient for users to get started.

Verification Process on Huobi Exchange

To verify your Huobi account, simply log into your account and click on the “Verification” option under your profile icon. It’s a straightforward process that only takes a few minutes to complete.

However, please note that in some cases, it might take up to 7 days for review. To complete the KYC verification necessary for depositing and trading on Huobi, you can go to the “Balances” section under your “Exchange Account.” On the Huobi mobile app, tap on “Me” at the bottom of the homepage and then select “Unverified” in the upper right corner to start the identity verification process.

Remember to carefully review all transaction information before submitting any instructions as Huobi will process transactions based on what they receive from you.

Begin Trading on Huobi Exchange

To begin trading on Huobi Exchange, you can follow a simple process. First, sign up for an account on the Huobi website and complete the verification process. Once your account is verified, you can deposit funds into your Huobi wallet using supported payment methods.

With funds in your wallet, you can explore the wide range of cryptocurrencies available on Huobi and choose the ones you want to trade. Simply select the desired cryptocurrency pair, enter the amount to buy or sell, review the details, and confirm your trade.

Huobi charges trading fees based on a maker-taker model where fees vary depending on whether you are adding liquidity (maker) or removing liquidity (taker). It’s important to note that before starting to trade, it’s recommended to do thorough research and consider factors such as market conditions and risk tolerance.
