Bitpanda Account Types

Bitpanda offers three different account types: Starter Level, Bronze Level, and Gold Level. Each level comes with its own set of benefits and features, allowing users to choose the one that best suits their needs.

If you want to learn more about these account types and how they can benefit you, continue reading this review.

Starter Level

The Starter Level account is one of the account types available on Bitpanda. This level is ideal for beginners who are new to cryptocurrency trading. With a Starter Level account, you can enjoy features such as buying and selling cryptocurrencies, depositing and withdrawing funds using various payment methods, and accessing the user-friendly interface of Bitpanda.

It’s a great option if you’re just starting out and want to explore the world of crypto in a simple and straightforward way. Additionally, verified account holders at this level can also use Bitpanda Pay to conveniently pay bills to any IBAN in the EU.

The Starter Level offers a beginner-friendly experience on Bitpanda with access to essential features for trading and managing your cryptocurrencies.

Bronze Level

The Bronze level is one of the account types offered by Bitpanda, a leading cryptocurrency broker. This account type has specific features and benefits designed to suit the needs of users at this level.

To create a Bronze level account, you will need to go through a verification process that requires your mobile number. It’s important to note that the starter account, which is another account type offered by Bitpanda, has a different verification process that is email-driven.

Bitpanda offers these different account types to cater to users at various levels of experience in trading cryptocurrencies.

Gold Level

To reach Gold Level on Bitpanda, you have to go through the longest verification process. It may take some time, but once you’re verified, you’ll have access to a range of exclusive benefits.

One of the perks is being able to pay bills with “Bitpanda Pay” to any IBAN in the EU. You can easily manage your expenses using your verified account. Another advantage is that you can invest in precious metals like gold, silver, palladium, and platinum.

This feature allows you to diversify your portfolio beyond just cryptocurrencies. So if you’re looking for more advanced options and want to expand your investment possibilities, reaching Gold Level on Bitpanda is definitely worth considering.

Bitpanda Ecosystem Token (BEST)

Bitpanda Ecosystem Token, also known as BEST, is a special token that operates on the Ethereum blockchain. It’s designed to give Bitpanda users exclusive benefits and rewards when they hold it.

By investing in BEST, you can unlock perks like lower trading fees and better exchange rates on the Bitpanda platform. Plus, if you’re an active trader, you have the opportunity to earn even more tokens annually.

In fact, customers can claim over 15% extra tokens every year just by holding BEST. So if you want to enhance your Bitpanda experience and enjoy these additional advantages, investing in BEST could be a smart move for you.

Bitpanda Customer Support

Bitpanda Customer Support is an area that could use improvement. Unfortunately, Bitpanda does not offer live chat or phone-based customer support. Instead, they direct customers to the FAQ section on their website for answers to common questions.

This means that if you have a specific issue or need personalized assistance, it may take longer to get a response. Some users have reported slow customer support from Bitpanda, which can be frustrating when you’re in need of help.

On the positive side, Bitpanda offers support for a wide range of payment methods and fiat currencies. They understand that different customers have different preferences when it comes to depositing and withdrawing funds, so they strive to accommodate as many options as possible.

Bitpanda provides over-the-counter services for customers with larger transactions who may require more personalized assistance.

While Bitpanda does provide some level of customer support through their FAQ section and email-based inquiries, it would be beneficial for them to invest in additional resources such as live chat or phone support to improve the overall customer experience.

Their infrastructure and systems use modern encryption technologies to ensure that your information is safe from unauthorized access. Bitpanda also has dedicated security teams working around the clock to monitor any suspicious activity and provide immediate support if needed.

To protect your personal data, Bitpanda uses SSL encryption, which keeps your information anonymous and secure while using their platform. They also implement additional safety measures to prevent any unauthorized access to your account.
