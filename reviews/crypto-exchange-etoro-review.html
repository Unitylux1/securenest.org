Pioneering the world of social trading, eToro was established in Israel in 2007. Its distinct approach to financial services revolutionized the Fintech industry and set it apart from traditional brokers.

Combining aspects of social networking with traditional trading, eToro opened its doors to both novices and seasoned traders alike. Over time, this innovative platform expanded beyond its Israeli borders, reaching various international markets while maintaining its headquarters in Central Israel.

Today, eToro is recognized as one of the first platforms that made a significant impact on how people trade by offering low-cost options for stocks, CFDs, forex trading and cryptocurrencies.

Account setup and verification process 

Setting up an account on eToro is a straightforward process, allowing users to start trading quickly and easily. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Visit the eToro website or download the mobile app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
  2. Click on the “Join Now” button to begin the registration process.
  3. You can create an account using your email address and password, or sign up with your existing Google or Facebook account for a faster registration.
  4. Once you’ve entered your details, click on “Create Account” to proceed.
  5. Next, you’ll need to verify your email address by clicking on the verification link sent to your inbox.
  6. After verifying your email, you’ll be prompted to complete the KYC (Know Your Customer) process to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements.
  7. To complete the KYC process, you’ll need to provide personal information such as your full name, date of birth, residential address, and phone number.
  8. You’ll also need to upload a valid government – issued ID document (e.g., passport or driver’s license) for identity verification purposes.
  9. Once all the required information and documents are submitted, eToro will review and verify your account within a few business days.
  10. Upon successful verification, you can fund your account by depositing funds using various payment methods available on the platform, such as credit/debit card or bank transfer.
  11. After funding your account, you can start exploring and trading cryptocurrencies, stocks, ETFs, and other assets offered by eToro.

Buying and selling cryptocurrencies on eToro 

To buy and sell cryptocurrencies on eToro, follow these simple steps:

  1. Sign up for an eToro account by providing your personal details and completing the verification process.
  2. Deposit funds into your eToro account using a credit card, bank transfer, or other available payment methods.
  3. Browse the list of available cryptocurrencies on the eToro platform.
  4. Choose the cryptocurrency you want to buy and click on it to access more information.
  5. Select the amount of cryptocurrency you wish to purchase and review the estimated cost, including fees.
  6. Click on the “Buy” button to confirm your purchase.
  7. To sell cryptocurrencies, go to your portfolio section and locate the cryptocurrency you want to sell.
  8. Click on it to access the options menu and select “Sell.”
  9. Enter the amount of cryptocurrency you want to sell and review the estimated proceeds after fees.
  10. Click on the “Sell” button to confirm your sale.
